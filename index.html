About Us

located in Historic Downtown Yankton, South Dakota where we have our state of the art screen printing and embroidery services as well as our CAD printing production! Our company is a proud supplier of logoed and blank apparel and promotional products. We supply companies in South Dakota as well as other states across the nation with logoed apparel that proudly advertises their businesses, events or team. We carry many name brand polos, t-shirts, jackets, and athletic apparel including Nike, Gildan, Port Authority, Jerzee, Eddie Bauer, Champion, Real Tree, Adidas, and many more.

Services Contact Us

Screen Printing

We offer whole sale screen printing services on a huge selection of brands. Just give us a call!

Embroidery

We also specialize in Embroidery! We embroider almost anything as long as it can fit on our machines and a needle can poke through it!

Promotional Products

We can work with you on getting your promotional products to help market your business! We have a wide selection ranging from mail openers, portable phone chargers to even lawn chairs!
